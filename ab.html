FAQs

1. Is homeopathy safe for long-term use?

Yes, when supervised by a doctor. Our products are crafted under GMP with clean labels. Always follow recommended guidance.

2. Can I take homeopathic and allopathic medicines together?

Often yes, but ask your doctor about everything you take.

3. How soon will I see results?

Some see relief in days; chronic issues may take longer. Root-cause healing is steady, sustainable, and personalized.

4. Are your products lab-tested?

Every batch is tested for identity, purity, and potency. We maintain transparent records and traceability.

5. Is homeopathy safe for pregnant women and breastfeeding mothers?

Yes, when used under proper doctor guidance. Homeopathy is considered safe during pregnancy and breastfeeding because remedies are very gentle. However, it is always best to consult a qualified doctor before use.
